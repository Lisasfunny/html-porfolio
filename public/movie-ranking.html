Alyssia's Movie rankings

Top movies of June 2023

1. Spider-man:Across The Spider-Verse

9/10

This was one of the most highly anticipated movies of the year, and I'm glad I got to experience it. The animation was just as flawless if not better than the first movie. I fell in love with all the characters and each one of their Unique styles. I also like the people they asked to voice act, a lot of these Characters. They were very inclusive with their choices. I can tell there was a lot of love put into this. It was fun, serious, and really pulled me in to the world of Spider-Man like never before. I could watch it over and over again.

2. The Boogeyman

8/10

This felt like a classic horror/ thriller film. If it had been made, a couple years ago, I know for It would have been the "scream" of its time. The premise isn't anything new, but it does do a nice just of making the Boogeyman Relevant again. It had some pretty wicked jump scares that I did come to expect after a while, but it was still fun all the same. I would totally watch it again maybe with someone who gets scared a little easier than me. Sidenote the little girl, unlike in most horror/thriller movies, was a total GOAT. Loved her.

3. Transformers:Rise of the Beasts

4/10

It just felt like any other Transformers movie expect with animals. Honestly, just felt like they were tring to sell toys rather than make a movie that the fans would want to watch.
